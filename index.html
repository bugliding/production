About Us

We are the Boston University Gliding and Soaring Club (BUGS), a student-led organization at Boston University. From designing styrofoam gliders to receiving flight instruction in our Stemme S6 self propelled glider, BUGS connects the academic pursuit of aviation with actual hands-on experience for students that extend far beyond the classroom.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make the world of aviation accessible to every Boston University student. We build a welcoming community of aviators, share the joy of flight, and open the door to gliding and soaring for those who never thought it was within reach.

Our Current Goal

This 2026-2027 academic year, we are working towards providing scholarships for Boston University students to take discovery flights, where they will have the option to then pursue flight training and obtain their Private Pilot License - Glider (PPL-G) in our Stemme S6.
